Top game apps

Here are some of the most popular game apps, and their download links.

  • Steam
    Steam is a platform for playing, creating, and discussing games. It has a huge community and tons of games, both free and paid. It supports Windows, MacOS, and Linux.
  • Epic Games
    Epic games is a platform for playing and creating games. It is the home of popular games like Fortnite, Rocket League, and Fall Guys. They have both free and paid games. It supports both Windows and MacOS.
  • Minecraft
    Minecraft is a hugely popular sandbox block game with singleplayer and multiplayer worlds. It is supported on Windows, MacOS, Linux, Android, iOS, Oculus, Amazon Fire, Xbox One, Xbox 360, Nintendo Switch, PS4, PS3, PSVITA, Wii U, and the Nintendo 3DS.
  • Roblox
    Roblox is a very popular game platform with millions of games and users. It has easy game creation as well through its app Roblox Studio. It is supported on MacOS, Windows, iOS, Android, and Xbox.

  • What's a game platform?

    A game platform is a platform with many different games for users to play under one account. Game platforms usually have their own engines for game creation, as well.
