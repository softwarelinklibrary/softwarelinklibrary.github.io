Top media players

Here are some of the most popular media players, and their download links.

  1. VLC Media Player
    VLC Media Player from VideoLAN is a hugely compatible and versatile media player with support for virtually all desktop operating systems, and most mobile operating systems. It has support for many inputs and filetypes, and allows interface changing through skins.
  2. Potplayer
    Potplayer has a multitude of advanced features for media consumption. It supports 3D video, DVD, HD TV, Bookmarks, and snapshots.
  3. Kodi
    Kodi is a media center program for Windows, MacOS, Linux, Android, Android TV, Google TV, Apple TV, and the Raspberry Pi. It supports add-ons for various features including PVR clients like Pluto TV and TVHeadend, YouTube (requiring an API key), and more. It provides an interface similar to most smart TVs, and supports playback of images, videos, and music.
  4. iTunes
    iTunes from Apple is a great way to purchase, and download music, and play and back up information/media from Apple devices.

Top media streaming apps

Here are some of the most popular media streaming apps, and their download links.

  1. Spotify
    Spotify is the most popular music streaming app. It offers free streaming with ads or more features with a Premium subscription. It allows for playlists, podcasts, radios, and lots more.
  2. Apple Music ($)
    Apple Music is Apple's music streaming platform. With a subscription you get access to lots of songs, radios, playlists and more.
  3. Amazon Music ($)
    Amazon Music is a music streaming platform from Amazon. It is included with Amazon Prime, but there are higher-tier subscriptions available with more features.

  • What's a media player?

    A media player allows the user to watch multiple image, video, and audio formats, usually including more features than the default media player included in their operating system.
