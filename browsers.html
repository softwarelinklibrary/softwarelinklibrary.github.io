Top browsers

Here are some of the most popular browsers, and their download links.

  1. Google Chrome
    Chrome is a browser by Google, and it's the most popular browser in the world, with about 65% of browser market share.
  2. Mozilla Firefox
    Firefox is a fast, open-source browser from Mozilla. It has about 4% of the browser market share.
  3. Brave
    Brave is a private browser focused primarily on speed and privacy. Brave has about 0.05% of browser market share as of early 2023.
  4. Opera
    Opera is a browser with a huge amount of features. A free, built-in VPN, integrated messaging platforms, media players in the sidebar, and a built in ad-blocker are just a few. Opera has about 2.5% of browser market share as of 2023.
  5. Opera GX
    Opera GX is a browser targeted at gamers. It has many of the same features as Opera, but with more controls. It includes built-in support for Twitch and Discord right in the sidebar, as well as the GX Corner which showcases games, including games with on-going sales.

  • What's a browser?

    A browser is a program which allows you to access websites on the internet. Microsoft bundles a browser with Windows called Microsoft Edge, but many people just download a different browser to use instead, like Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox. You're using a browser to access this website right now.
